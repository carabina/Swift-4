PlatformClient

public final class PlatformClient : RemoteService

Provides functionality for the Platform API, which supports general configuration and information support for cannabis retail partner locations. Domains are resolved through the Platform Service to their respective partner and location account codes, or domain info can be resolved for a given partner/location pair. Other utility methods are supported through the Platform Service including healthchecks, pings, and others.
